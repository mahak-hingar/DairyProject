Welcome to our Dairy

Towards fulfillment of the national objective of making India self sufficient in milk production, a small step was taken in July and Jaipur Zila Dugdh Utpadak Sahakari Sangh Ltd., Jaipur was registered under Cooperative Act 1965 to work in then Jaipur District. Initially this union did not have the processing facilities. It started with a modest beginning of procuring 250 liters of milk per day.

About Us

At this dairy, our humble mission is to serve cows with love and devotion. In the process, we engage in protecting cows, promoting the growth of desi cows and their products, and helping farmers to get back to organic farming with the help of cows. This enables us to serve our customers with traditional dairy and food products leading us to a happier and healthier lifestyle.
